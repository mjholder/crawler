## What I worked on

Fixed rapid-attack bug: player could press the attack button multiple times during a single turn, registering multiple attacks before `turn_ended` fired. Added `_turn_pending` to the guard clause in `player.gd:execute_action()` so only one action is accepted per turn.

Fixed skeleton instant-death bug: `skeleton.gd:_on_ready()` was overriding the base `enemy._on_ready()` without calling `super._on_ready()`. This meant `health` was never initialized (stayed at 0.0) and the health bar was never wired to the `damaged` signal. Skeletons died in one hit regardless of damage. Added `super._on_ready()` call — enemies now start at full HP and health bars update on damage.
